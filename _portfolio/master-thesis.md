My master thesis was completed at ÉTS and nominated for the ÉTS Prix d'excellence.

This work contributed to my research path in generative AI, diffusion models, efficient adaptation, and applied machine learning. It also shaped my current interest in developing generative methods that can move from academic prototypes to practical and production-oriented systems.

The thesis helped define the research direction I continue to pursue today: high-fidelity generative systems that are not only scientifically interesting, but also robust and usable in applied settings.
