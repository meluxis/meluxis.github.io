Uni-DAD proposes a unified framework for jointly adapting and distilling diffusion models for few-step, few-shot image generation.

This work focuses on efficient generative adaptation, combining diffusion model adaptation with few-step generation to improve the practicality of text-to-image generation under limited-domain data.
