Uni-DAD introduces a unified single-stage framework for jointly adapting and distilling diffusion models for few-step, few-shot image generation.

This work focuses on efficient generative adaptation under limited target-domain data. By combining diffusion model adaptation with few-step generation in a single framework, it aims to make high-quality text-to-image generation more practical for real-world, data-constrained settings.
